Case Study · Logistics

Near-zero downtime for a nationwide logistics platform.

A fast-growing logistics operator was losing orders every time their ageing platform stalled. We rebuilt the core, and the outages stopped.

The problem

Every peak season, the platform buckled. Orders timed out, drivers lost their routes, and the operations team spent nights firefighting instead of shipping. The system had grown by accretion over a decade — no one person understood all of it, and every fix risked breaking something else.

What we did

We started by mapping the whole system end to end before touching code, so we understood exactly where it strained. Then a small senior team rebuilt the core order pipeline, moved it onto an auto-scaling cloud foundation, and put real-time monitoring in front of everything.

  • Re-architected the order pipeline for back-pressure and graceful failure
  • Migrated to an auto-scaling, hardened cloud environment
  • Built a real-time dashboard so operations can see issues before customers do
  • Set up automated backups and a tested disaster-recovery path
“For the first time in years, peak season was boring. Nothing fell over.” — Head of Operations

The result

The platform now holds 99.98% uptime through peak load, incidents dropped by over 90%, and the operations team is back to growing the business instead of nursing the system. We continue to maintain and harden it — the same people who built it.
