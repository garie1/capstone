Closer to where the work is

Demand from Southeast Asia has grown steadily, and our clients there increasingly want engineers in a compatible time zone. So we’ve opened a second delivery pod in Singapore — a small, senior team that operates to exactly the same standard as Hong Kong.

Same people, same standard

This isn’t an outsourced satellite. It’s staffed by senior engineers who own work end to end, the same way we always have. The two pods share tooling, review practices, and on-call so a client never feels a seam between them.

  • Senior engineers, no junior hand-offs
  • Overlapping coverage across HK & SG time zones
  • Shared architecture and review standards
  • One accountable team, two locations
“Now there’s always someone senior awake when our systems need them.” — Regional Client (sample quote)

What’s next

The Singapore pod is already supporting live engagements. If you’re building in the region and want a senior team in your time zone, we’d be glad to talk.
